Privacy Policy

QBO Farm Dashboard is a private internal farm management dashboard for Pustejovsky & Sons. It connects to QuickBooks Online only with authorized user consent.

The app accesses QuickBooks Online accounting data only after authorization. Data is used only for internal farm reporting, budgeting, liability payment review, and break-even analysis.

Data is not sold. Data is not shared with advertisers. This site does not use tracking scripts or external analytics.

Tokens are stored locally by the user's app instance. Generated reports remain local unless the user chooses to share them.

The user may disconnect the app through Intuit/QuickBooks app connections.
